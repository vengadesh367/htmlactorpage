vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, popularly known as Vijay or "Thalapathy," is a leading Indian actor, playback singer, and politician primarily associated with Tamil cinema. Early Life and Family Vijay was born on 22 June 1974 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, to S. A. Chandrasekhar, a film director, and Shoba Chandrasekhar, a playback singer and Carnatic vocalist. He had a younger sister, Vidhya, who passed away at the age of two, and his production house, V.V Productions, is named in her memory. Vijay grew up in a Roman Catholic family and began acting as a child artist in his father’s films, debuting in Vetri (1984)
